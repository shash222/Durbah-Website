Element How to find it
Flyer Location
  1. Go to AlMaghrib.org/ and click on "Upcoming Events"
  2. Select the appropriate location (New Jersey)
  3. Right click on the flyer and select "Copy image address" and paste into form below
Promo Video URL
  1. Go to AlMaghrib.org and click on "Upcoming Events"
  2. Select the appropriate location (New Jersey) then click on "More Info"
  3. Right click on Youtube Video and select "Copy video URL" and paste into form below
Registration Link
  1. This should be location page (AlMaghrib.org/NewJersey)
About Instructor Text
  1. Go to AlMaghrib.org/ and click on "Instructors"
  2. Click on desired instructor and copy whatever portion of Instructor Bio to be displayed on website
Link to More About Instructor
  1. Go to AlMaghrib.org/ and click on "Instructors"
  2. Click on desired instructor and copy URL in address box and paste into form below
Link to Instructor Image
  1. Go to AlMaghrib.org/ and click on "Instructors"
  2. Click on desired instructor
  3. Right click on instructor image (may need to scroll down a bit) and select "Copy image address"
  4. Paste into form below
